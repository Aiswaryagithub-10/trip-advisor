Suggestions

Get the beautiful memories with travel

Alleppey

Alleppey is home to the longest and largest snake boat races in India

Munnar

Munnar is home to the highest tea plantations in the world, offering stunning views of lush green hills and misty valleys.

Thekkady

Thekkady offers a unique chance to spot wildlife while boating on Periyar Lake.

>

Kochi

Kochi is known for its iconic Chinese fishing nets, a symbol of the city's rich maritime history.

Kovalam

Kovalam is famous for its crescent-shaped beaches, ideal for surfing and sunset views.

Wayanad

Wayanad's Love Lake, also known as Pookode Lake, is a serene, heart-shaped lake surrounded by lush forests.

Kozhikode

Kozhikode is known for its iconic Calicut Halwa, a popular sweet treat with a unique taste.

Kannur

Kannur boasts the beautiful Parassinikadavu Muthappan Temple, known for its unique Theyyam performances.
